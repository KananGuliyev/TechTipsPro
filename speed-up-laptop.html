5 Easy Ways to Speed Up Your Laptop (2025 Guide)

Is your laptop feeling slow? Try these tips to make it faster without spending money:

  1. Uninstall Unused Programs: Remove software you don’t use anymore.
  2. Disable Startup Apps: Prevent unnecessary apps from launching at startup.
  3. Clean Your Hard Drive: Use built-in tools like Disk Cleanup or Storage Sense.
  4. Update Drivers and OS: Outdated drivers can slow down performance.
  5. Use Lightweight Alternatives: Try lighter browsers like Brave or Opera instead of Chrome.
